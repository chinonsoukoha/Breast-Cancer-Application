How to Use the Breast Cancer Diagnosis Tool

With a Laptop or Desktop

Connect your laptop/desktop to your microscope using a Microscope USB and take a picture of the breast histopathological image. Here are some useful links to give more details:

  • Stereo Microscope
  • Digital Microscope

Upload your picture to the Melignant App and view the result.

With your Smartphone

Use your phone to take a picture of the breast cancer histopathological image through your microscopic lens. Here are some useful links to give more details:

  • Video 1
  • Video 2

Upload your picture to the Melignant App and view the result.
