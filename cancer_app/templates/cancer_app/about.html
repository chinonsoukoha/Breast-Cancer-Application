About MELIGNANT

Melignant is a breast cancer diagnosis system designed by Ukoha Chinonso Precious as her Final Year Project. It was designed to combat the problem of lack of pathologists, misdiagnosis of breast cancer and lack of available datasets for research purposes in Nigeria and possibly other Sub-Saharan African countries. Melignant helps pathologists:

  • Diagnose breast cancer histopathological images.
  • Perform a risk assessment test on patients that have benign tumors in order to detect their risk status and proffer preventive solutions
  • Create a database of indigenous histopathological images which can be used for further research

Please note that Melignant is to be used as a second option to an actual pathologist diagnosis.
